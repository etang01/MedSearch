Common Cold

Description

The common cold is a viral infection of your nose and throat (upper respiratory tract). It's usually harmless, although it might not feel that way. Many types of viruses can cause a common cold.

Symptoms

Continuous sneezing, Chills, Fatigue, Cough, High fever, Headache, Swelled lymphnodes, Malaise, Phlegm, Throat irritation, Redness of eyes, Sinus pressure, Runny nose, Congestion, Chest pain, Loss of smell, Muscle pain

What To Do

1. Drink vitamin C rich drinks

2. Take vapour

3. Avoid cold food

4. Keep fever in check
