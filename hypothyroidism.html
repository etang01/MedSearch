Hypothyroidism

Description

Hypothyroidism, also called underactive thyroid or low thyroid, is a disorder of the endocrine system in which the thyroid gland does not produce enough thyroid hormone.

Symptoms

Fatigue, weight gain, cold hands and feets, mood swings, lethargy, dizziness, puffy face and eyes, enlarged thyroid, brittle nails, swollen extremeties, depression, irritability, abnormal menstruation

What To Do

1. Reduce stress

2. Exercise

3. Eat healthy

4. Get proper sleep
